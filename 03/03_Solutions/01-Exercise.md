### Exercise 1: Initialize Docker Swarm service
####  Pre requisit
1. Multipass has been successfully installed.
2. You've a pushed image in your Docker-Hub account.
#### Tasks
1. Create a Bash script called ```setup-service.sh``` for 
setting up a Docker Swarm service based on your containerized/pulled app.
2. Transfer the script to your leader node.
3. Check if it runs error free. In case of errors: 
   - Analyze the error message
   - Figure out the cause of the error and test an initial workaround (troubleshooting).
